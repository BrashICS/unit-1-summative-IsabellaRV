Who was Konrad Zuse?

Full name Konrad Ernst Otto Zuse, Konrad was a German civil engineer who is primarily known for creating the first programmable computer.

He was born in Berlin, Germany on June 22, 1910 and attended the Collegium Hosianum in Braunsberg. He enrolled at the Technische Hochschule Berlin and studied both engineering and architecture, which he found incredibly boring. Zuse graduated from civil engineering in 1939.
